    Class PackageConnectorHttpRest

    Holds a live-link to AAS exposing the (standadized) HTTP REST interface. Note: as of Jan 2021, the REST routes of contemporary aasx-server (prototype AO, MIHO) are being used. In a later stage, this shall switch to the "official" HTPP REST routes of AASiD.
